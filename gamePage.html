    • DOSGames.com Review:

    Alex the Allegator 2 is a puzzle game similar to Othello or other knock-offs like Hexxagon but it's gameplay mechanic is different enough that it stands on its own. The object is to complete squares of four blocks by placing your tokens on the board or sliding a row or column. The sliding mechanic is new and changes up the game considerably. The graphics and gameplay work well, and use of the mouse is appreciated. One or two player mode is supported. This game was originally made for a 72 hour game competition.
