    • DOSGames.com Review:

    An excellent conversion of the old arcade classic. If you've never played the original, this was the introduction of Mario (then called "Jumpman") and the gameplay consists of a variety of platforming levels where the goal is to rescue Pauline from the great ape. It features great graphics, sweet SB sound (taken directly from the arcade!) and lots of other nice, professional touches. Overall ... it's a great recreation of the arcade, which also includes a "CHAMP" mode with additional content.
